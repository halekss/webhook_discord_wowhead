In Delves,  Lightsmith will be the better option because it provides more passive self-healing and defensiveness. You want to use as many of your resources, spells, and cooldowns to deal damage as possible. Keep a Beacon on yourself and use the occasional  Infusion of Light proc to cast a powerful  Flash of Light on yourself as needed.
